HM70 — Built for the Field

Compact, dependable imaging at the bedside and in the field. The HM70 is the system Samsung kept available specifically for markets like West Africa — portable, proven, and priced to reach more clinics without compromising on image quality.

  • Crystal Architecture — Samsung's CrystalBeam + CrystalLive imaging engine for sharper, more consistent image quality in any setting.
  • S-Vue Transducer — Multi-frequency probes purpose-built for Point-of-Care workflows, from abdominal to cardiac and vascular.
  • IntelligentAssist — AI-guided image optimization and auto-measurements to reduce operator dependency.
  • MV-Flow — Micro-vascular flow imaging for detecting subtle blood flow without the limitations of standard color Doppler.
